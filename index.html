FridgeSnap

FridgeSnap is a mobile application designed to reduce food waste. With FridgeSnap, you are now able to find recipes and ideas for leftover food items! Anything from bananas to sausages to eggs, FridgeSnap has an idea for it!
FridgeSnap was developed using numerous Machine Learning and Image Classification libraries and was trained upon a large food item dataset
